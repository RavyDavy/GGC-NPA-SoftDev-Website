Cafe

Our Cafe is open at the same times as our visitor centre. i.e Monday to Saturday - 8am to 5pm and Sundays - 9am to 4.30pm.

Whether you just want a coffee & a cake, or a full blown meal and a dessert, we have menus to suit all situations.

The Cafe is available for walk-ins only (i.e we don't take bookings), but there are plenty of seating both inside and out.

We are also happy to cater to a variety of dietary requirements from vegan to veggie, and from gluten-free to dairy-free. Just let us know when you make your order, so we can advise you on the options.

Check out the menus on this page, so you know what we offer before your visit.
