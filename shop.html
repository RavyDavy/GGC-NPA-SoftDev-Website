Our Farm Shop

Our Farm Shop is open at the same times as our visitor centre. i.e Monday to Saturday - 8am to 5pm and Sundays - 9am to 4.30pm.

We are really proud of our large, well-stocked shop, full of local fresh produce.

Unforunately, we don't do online ordering yet, but we are planning to offer it in the near future!

Check out the listings on this page, so you know what we offer before your visit.

Fruit and Vegetables

  • Root Vegetables - Potatoes, Turnips, Carrots, Parsnips

  • Other Vegetables - Cabbage, Cauliflower, Broccoli, Sprouts, Peas, Leeks

  • Fruits - Blackberries, Blueberries, Raspberries, Strawberries, Gooseberries, Apples, Rhubarb

Fresh Produce

  • Dairy - Milk, Cream, Yogurt, Cheese, Butter

  • Eggs - Freshly Laid Orangic Free Range

  • Bakery & Sweets - Bread, Cakes, Hand-made Chocolate

  • Honey & Preserves - Honey from one of our beekeeper neighbours, and various flavours of jams, marmalades and chutneys

Butchery - Fresh Meats and Poultry

  • Meat Cuts
    • Beef - All your usual cuts of beef, including Sirloin, Ribs, Brisket, and more.

    • Pork - All you usual cuts of pork, including Belly, Loin, Shoulder and more.

    • Poultry - Including Chicken, Turkey and Duck.

  • Pre-packaged
    • Sausages - Pork, Beef in both Links and Lorne available.

    • Burgers - Selection of different types, including Ultimate Burger (6OZ), Cheese Burger, Cajun Chicken Burger and more.

    • Smoken Bacon - Selection of different types, including Streaky Bacon, Smoked Back Bacon, Sweetcure Back Bacon and more.
