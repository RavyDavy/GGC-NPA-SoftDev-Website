Activities

We have tons to do at our Farm Visitor Centre. Whether you want to spend a few hours, or the entire day, we are sure you won't be disappointed.

Apart from our main activities (see below for their descriptions), we also have a hedge maze, and we do pony rides. We also cater for rainy weather with our large activity barn.



Seasonal Activities

We have special events throughout the year, so please join us in our Santa's Grotto, or join us in our Big Easter Hunt!

Check out our Seasonal Activities here.

Adventure Playpark

Our Adventure Playpark has a ton of exciting equipment in it, suitable for kids of all ages.

It has all the usual things like swings, chutes, seasaws and roundabouts, but it also has more exciting things such as a rope climbing frame, sandpit diggers, and our favourite: The Flying Fox zipwire!

Petting Zoo

Our Petting Zoo is one of our most popular attractions, and we have a large range of different animals available to view and pet*, including sheep, goats, pigs, Highland cows, deer, donkeys, horses, chickens, ducks, turkeys, rhea, buinea pigs, rabbits, wallabies, and llamas.

The zoo is split between our outdoor area, and our indoor barn, which is perfect for when it rains.

Our Petting Zoo is our only chargable attraction, prices are as follows:

  • Adults: £5 each
  • Children - aged 4-16: £3 each
  • Children - under 4: free
Please phone us on 01864 502 501 to book tickets.

* Please note that although all of our animals are tame, there are certain occasions when they don't want petted, such as when newborns are around. We have appropriate signage when this is the case.

Forest Trail Walks

We have 3 main forest trail walks starting from the Farm. They are classed as Easy, Medium, and Challenging, depending on the distance each walk actually is.

The Easy Walk is around 3 miles. The Medium Walk is around 5 miles, and our Challenging Walk is around 10 miles.

All of our walks take in the beautiful scenary of the countryside around our Farm.

Please wear sensible shoes for doing any of the walks, including waterproof boots or wellies for wet weather.
