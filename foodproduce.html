Food & Produce

As we are a working farm, we have a lot of animals and crops including fruit and vegetables, which we use in both our cafe and our shop. We also work along with our neighbours to obtain and sell other products too.



Cafe

All the ingredients on our cafe menu are from either from our own farm, or sourced locally from the community. We can guarantee that everything is fresh, tasty, with no additives, preservatives, or anything artifial.
Just home grown good food.
